  Text boxes: 3
  [1] Section heading — (e.g., "Session Goals" or "Therapist Guide"), Cormorant Garamond
  [2] Subheading — (e.g., "Talking Points" or "Clinical Notes"), DM Sans bold
  [3] Body content — bullet list or paragraph, DM Sans regular

TEMPLATE D — EXERCISE PAGE
  Text boxes: 4
  [1] Exercise title with timing — (e.g., "IN-SESSION EXERCISE: Body Map (30 min)")

  [3] Numbered steps — body text, DM Sans
  [4] Facilitator note or tip — small callout box at bottom
  Design note: Number each step clearly, consider light background for materials box

TEMPLATE E — CLIENT TAKE-HOME / WORKSHEET PAGE

  Design note: Leave generous white space for writing. This is the page clients
  will actually fill out.

CANVA TIPS
================================================================================

1. CREATE 7 MASTER TEMPLATES FIRST
   Build Templates A through G (listed above) as your first 7 pages in Canva.
   Then duplicate and fill as you go. This saves enormous time.

2. CONSISTENT SPACING
   - Top margin: 0.75 in
   - Side margins: 1 in
   - Between text boxes: 0.3-0.5 in
   - Bottom margin: 0.75 in

3. TEXT SIZES (recommended)
   - Session number: 16-18pt DM Sans, gold color
   - Session title: 32-36pt Cormorant Garamond
   - Section headings: 22-26pt Cormorant Garamond
   - Subheadings: 14-16pt DM Sans bold
   - Body text: 11-12pt DM Sans
   - Journaling prompts: 12pt DM Sans italic
   - Modality tags: 10pt DM Sans, uppercase

4. VISUAL CONSISTENCY
   - Session divider pages: cream/warm background with gold accent bar
   - Exercise pages: white background with sage left border
   - Take-home pages: white background with gold left border
   - Group adaptation pages: light sage tint background
   - Journaling prompts: always in a callout box with gold or sage border

5. FILLABLE ELEMENTS
   For take-home worksheets, use:
   - Dotted lines for short answers (______)
   - Lined boxes for longer writing
   - Circle/checkbox elements for rating scales
   - Table grids for logs and trackers

6. OPTIONAL ADDITIONS
   - Decorative botanical/organic illustrations between sections
   - Small modality icons next to tags (brain for CBT, palette for Expressive Arts,
     body for Somatic, puzzle for IFS)
   - Pull quotes from the curriculum in accent boxes
   - Page numbers in footer (DM Sans, 9pt, centered)
