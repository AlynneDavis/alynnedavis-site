Heal Your Relationship with Food for a Healthier Body, Mind and Life

In this episode, Alynne talks about what it really means to heal your relationship with food — moving beyond diet culture, understanding the emotional and trauma roots of disordered eating, and what a whole-person approach to recovery actually looks like. If you've ever felt stuck in cycles with food or your body, this conversation is for you.

Voice of the Voiceless

How Expressive Arts Therapy Uncovers Trauma Hidden in the Mind's Shadow

Expressive Arts Therapy Trauma Healing The Shadow Self Somatic Healing Women's Mental Health

In this conversation, Alynne explores how expressive arts therapy reaches the parts of us that words alone can't access — the unconscious patterns, suppressed memories, and hidden emotional wounds that live in the "shadow." A deeply honest conversation about what trauma really looks like inside the body and mind, and why creative expression can be such a powerful path to healing.
