Therapy Services

Individual therapy, expressive arts sessions, and intensive programs designed to support women through eating disorders, trauma, ADHD, and life transitions in Charlotte, NC.

How We Can Work Together

I offer several pathways into therapeutic work, each tailored to meet you where you are. Whether you're looking for weekly support, an immersive experience, or a structured program you can do from home, there's a way forward that fits your life.

Individual Therapy

Weekly 50-minute sessions — the foundation of our work together. We'll use a blend of CBT, Internal Family Systems, and somatic awareness to address the patterns, beliefs, and experiences that are keeping you stuck.

In-person in Charlotte, NC or virtual throughout NC and SC.

Expressive Arts Therapy

Creative therapeutic sessions incorporating painting, movement, journaling, and music to access emotions and experiences that talk therapy alone may not reach. No artistic skill or experience is needed.

Available as a standalone service or integrated into individual therapy.

The Self-Discovery Intensive

A comprehensive 3-day assessment program using the MaRI® assessment framework. This immersive experience provides deep insight into your patterns, strengths, and areas for growth — ideal for women ready to accelerate their self-understanding.

In-person in Charlotte, NC.

Coming Home to Self

A 12-week online program integrating CBT, Internal Family Systems, and expressive arts therapy. Designed for women who want a structured, guided path toward reconnecting with themselves — on their own schedule, from anywhere.

Self-paced online program with guided support.

Specializations

My clinical work is focused on the issues that most deeply affect women — and that require specialized training, not just general talk therapy. I bring more than nine years of clinical experience to each of these areas.

Eating Disorders

Specialized treatment for binge eating disorder, bulimia, ARFID (Avoidant/Restrictive Food Intake Disorder), orthorexia, and disordered eating patterns. I understand the complexity of food, control, and identity — and I approach recovery with both clinical expertise and deep compassion.

Trauma and PTSD

Trauma-informed therapy for women navigating the effects of childhood experiences, relational trauma, complex PTSD, and adverse life events. We work at a pace that honors your nervous system and builds genuine safety from the inside out.

ADHD in Women

ADHD in women is frequently undiagnosed or masked by anxiety, perfectionism, and people-pleasing. I help women understand their ADHD, develop self-compassion, and build strategies that work with their brain rather than against it.

Body Image

Healing your relationship with your body is central to healing your relationship with yourself. I support women in moving from chronic self-monitoring and body shame toward embodiment, acceptance, and trust.

Perinatal Mood Disorders

Pregnancy and postpartum bring enormous emotional shifts that deserve specialized support. I work with women experiencing perinatal anxiety, depression, and the identity changes that come with becoming a mother.

Life Transitions

Career changes, relationship shifts, loss, identity questions, and the quiet crises that don't always have a name — I support women through the transitions that shake the ground beneath them and help them find solid footing again.

Women's Mental Health

From perfectionism and burnout to self-worth and relational patterns, I understand the unique pressures women face. My practice offers a space to process, heal, and grow without performing or pretending.

Session Details and Logistics

In-Person and Virtual Options

I offer both in-person sessions at my Charlotte, NC office and virtual therapy sessions for clients located anywhere in North Carolina and South Carolina. Virtual sessions are conducted through a secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth platform, providing the same quality of care from the comfort of your own space.

Insurance and Payment

I am an out-of-network provider. This means I do not bill insurance directly, but I can provide a superbill (detailed receipt) that you can submit to your insurance company for potential out-of-network reimbursement. Many clients are surprised to find they receive significant reimbursement through their plans.

I accept payment via credit card, debit card, and HSA/FSA cards. Payment is due at the time of each session.

If cost is a concern, I encourage you to reach out. I want to make sure the right support is accessible to you.
