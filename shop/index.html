The Journal

Soul Nourishment

365 Days to Heal Your Relationship With Food and Self

This journal was created for the woman who wants a kinder, more connected, and more truthful relationship with food, her body, and her inner world. Written and designed by Alynne Davis, LCMHC — Expressive Arts Therapist and Soul System Practitioner — it blends trauma-informed guidance, daily intentions, expressive writing, and gentle self-inquiry.

Each of the 365 pages invites you to slow down, reconnect, and nourish the parts of you that have long needed softness and safety. Whether you are healing from disordered eating, emotional eating, body image struggles, or chronic self-criticism, this journal will meet you with warmth, compassion, and depth.

  • Daily Morning Intention — a grounding practice to begin each day with clarity and connection
  • Daily Healing Prompt — trauma-informed questions exploring the emotional and somatic patterns beneath your relationship with food
  • Evening Reflection — a gentle space to process your day and integrate your insights
  • Notes & Journaling Space — room for expressive arts, deeper writing, and reflective practice
