Embodied Reclamation Institute

1:1 Embodied Reclamation Coaching

Private, personalized coaching for women who are ready to do the deeper work — and finally come home to themselves.

Schedule a Discovery Call

You don’t need another strategy. Another framework to follow from the outside in.

What you need is someone who can help you see the story you’ve been living inside of — and walk with you as you find your way back to who you actually are.

That is what this work is.

1:1 Embodied Reclamation Coaching is the most intimate way to move through the RRR Method. It is personalized, somatic, and built entirely around you — your history, your nervous system, your pace, your becoming.

Available virtually to women worldwide, and in person in Charlotte, NC.

This Might Be For You If

  • You feel like you’ve been living someone else’s version of your life and you’re ready to find your own
  • You’re in a major life transition — divorce, career shift, midlife, identity upheaval — and you want to use it as a catalyst, not just survive it
  • You’ve done therapy and it helped, but you’re ready for something that goes beyond symptom management into full identity reclamation
  • You carry old stories about who you are — not good enough, too much, not enough, broken — and you’re ready to lay them down
  • You want to reconnect with your body, your creativity, your voice, and your sense of self in a way that actually lasts

What This Work Looks Like

The RRR Method in Private Practice

Every coaching relationship moves through the three phases of the RRR Method — though never in a rigid, linear way. Your path is your own.

Phase 01

Remember

We begin by getting honest about the narrative. The stories, the attachment patterns, the shame that has quietly been running things. This is not about rehashing the past — it is about finally seeing it clearly so it no longer runs you from the shadows.

Phase 02

Return

We bring you back into your body and your life. Somatic and expressive practices, attention to foundational nourishment — sleep, self-care, creativity, nervous system regulation — and the slow, intentional rebuilding of capacity that survival mode depleted.

Phase 03

Reclaim

This is where integration happens. Your voice returns. Your sense of self solidifies. You begin to move through your relationships, your work, and your daily life as who you actually are — not who you learned to be.

What Makes This Different

Clinical Depth. Coaching Container.

This is not productivity coaching. It is not mindset work dressed up in wellness language.

This is clinical-depth coaching led by someone who has spent over a decade in the room with women navigating trauma, body struggles, neurodivergence, and identity loss — and who has built a methodology from that work.

You will be guided by an embodied leader who holds:

  • Licensure as a Clinical Mental Health Counselor (LCMHC, LPC)
  • Training as an Expressive Arts Therapist
  • Certification as a MARI Practitioner
  • Certification as a Nutritionist
  • Healing Embodied Practitioner certification (in progress — international)

This depth of training means the work is nuanced, trauma-aware, and held with genuine clinical sophistication — while remaining clearly within a coaching container.

Please note: Coaching is not therapy and does not constitute mental health treatment. If you are seeking therapy services, please visit Alynne Davis, PLLC.

The Experience

What to Expect

When you work with me 1:1 you can expect:

  • A relationship built on deep presence, not a checklist
  • Sessions that move between conversation, somatic practice, creative expression, and reflection — whatever serves you in that moment
  • Personalized attention to what your nervous system and body are telling us, not just what your mind reports
  • A pace that honors where you are while gently expanding what’s possible
  • A guide who has done her own reclamation work and brings that lived knowledge into the room

This is not a passive process. It asks something of you. And it gives back more than you can imagine right now.

In the Spirit of Honesty

Who This Is Not For

This work is not the right fit if you are currently in acute mental health crisis, need a clinical diagnosis or psychiatric support, or are looking for quick fixes or surface-level life hacks.

If you are unsure whether coaching or therapy is the right next step, I’m happy to talk it through with you on a discovery call.
