Embodied Reclamation Institute

Welcome to the
Embodied Reclamation Institute

A body of work built for women who are ready to stop surviving and start remembering who they actually are.

Learn About the RRR Method Work With Me

What This Is

There is a version of you that existed before the survival patterns took over.

Before the shame stories. Before the attachment wounds shaped how you moved through the world. Before you learned to shrink, perform, over-function, or disappear just to feel safe.

She didn’t go anywhere. She’s been waiting.

The Embodied Reclamation Institute exists for one reason: to give you a structured, somatic, and deeply personal path back to her.

This is not about fixing what’s broken. You are not broken.

This is about remembering.

Who This Is For

You might be ready for this work if…

  • ✦ You’ve done the therapy, read the books, and still feel like something essential is missing
  • ✦ You know your patterns intellectually but can’t seem to shift them in your body
  • ✦ You’ve spent so long taking care of everyone else that you’ve lost the thread back to yourself
  • ✦ You’re in a season of transition — divorce, midlife, identity shift — and you’re ready to use it as a portal, not just survive it
  • ✦ You want to stop managing your life and start living it

The Framework at the Heart of Everything

The RRR Method

The RRR Method is an embodied framework developed from over a decade of clinical work with women navigating trauma, body struggles, neurodivergence, and life transitions. It is not a quick fix. It is a process of excavation, return, and integration — one that honors the intelligence of your nervous system, your body, and your story.

Phase 01

Remember

Awareness & Story

“You cannot reclaim what you cannot see.”This is where we begin — by identifying the narrative you’ve been living inside of. The attachment patterns that formed in childhood. The shame stories that disguised your truest self from you. The survival adaptations that kept you safe once but may be keeping you small now. Remember is not about blame or rehashing the past. It’s about seeing clearly — perhaps for the first time — what has been shaping you.

Phase 02

Return

Embodiment & Rebuilding Capacity

“You come home to yourself through your body, not around it.”After years of survival mode, many women find they have been living from the neck up — disconnected from the body that carries everything. Return is the phase of coming back. This includes nervous system regulation, nourishment, sleep, self-care, creative expression, and somatic practices that rebuild the capacity that depletion took. You don’t think your way back. You feel your way back.

Phase 03

Reclaim

Integration & Identity

“This is where the work becomes your life.”Reclaim is integration — bringing everything home. Your voice. Your body. Your relationships. Your sense of self — not who you were told to be, but who you actually are beneath all of it. This is not a return to who you were before. It is the emergence of who you have always been becoming.

Who Built This — and Why

About the Founder

I’m Alynne Davis — embodied leader, founder of the Embodied Reclamation Institute, and creator of the RRR Method.

I am a Licensed Clinical Mental Health Counselor, Licensed Professional Counselor, Expressive Arts Therapist, Certified MARI Practitioner, Certified Nutritionist, and Healing Embodied Practitioner (certification in progress). I have spent over a decade sitting with women in their most tender and transformational moments.

What I know — from the clinical work, from the certifications, and from my own reclamation — is that healing is not a cognitive event. It is an embodied one.

The RRR Method is the framework that emerged from all of it. Not a formula I found somewhere, but a map I built from the terrain I’ve walked with hundreds of women and within myself.

The Embodied Reclamation Institute is the body of work that holds it all — and it’s just beginning.
