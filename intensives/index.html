The Self-Discovery Intensive

A 3-session immersive assessment experience for women ready to go deeper — faster.

What Is the Self-Discovery Intensive?

The Self-Discovery Intensive is a guided 3-session experience designed for women who are ready to understand themselves on a deeper level. Each session is approximately 2 hours, spread across a flexible schedule that works for your life — sessions can be scheduled across consecutive weeks, or two in one week with the third the following week.

This intensive is built around the MARI® (Mandala Assessment Research Instrument), a clinically validated assessment tool that uses creative art expression and symbolic imagery to access parts of your inner world that words alone may not reach. The MARI® provides a non-verbal pathway to self-understanding — no artistic skill is needed, only a willingness to explore.

Whether you're at the beginning of your healing journey and want clarity on where to start, or you've been in therapy before and are looking for a deeper, more comprehensive picture of yourself — this intensive meets you where you are.

What to Expect Over 3 Sessions

Each session builds on the one before, creating a layered and thorough understanding of who you are beneath the patterns you've developed to cope.

  • Session 1 — The MARI® Assessment: Using the Mandala Assessment Research Instrument, you'll engage in creative art expression and work with symbolic imagery to access your inner landscape non-verbally. This is a powerful way to uncover patterns, emotions, and parts of yourself that may not surface through talk alone.
  • Session 2 — Discovery and Mapping: Together we'll review what was discovered in the MARI® assessment, explore the themes and symbols that emerged, and map out the internal parts that arrived through the process. This is where the picture of your inner world begins to come into focus.
  • Session 3 — Integration and Moving Forward: In our final session, we integrate the insights from the first two days through somatic work and embodied practices. You'll leave with a personalized plan for moving forward and a Self-Discovery Journal to continue the journey on your own — for ongoing self-discovery, reflection, and recognizing your progress over time.

What You'll Take Home

In addition to the deep personal insight gained through the process, you'll receive a Self-Discovery Journal that accompanies you throughout the intensive and beyond. This journal is designed to support continued self-exploration, help you track patterns and growth, and serve as a touchstone for the work you've done long after the intensive is complete.

Who Is This For?

The Self-Discovery Intensive may be a good fit if you:

  • Want a comprehensive understanding of your emotional and relational patterns
  • Are curious about non-verbal, creative approaches to self-understanding
  • Have been in therapy before but feel like something deeper hasn't been addressed
  • Are navigating a major life transition and need clarity
  • Struggle with perfectionism, self-doubt, or disconnection from yourself
  • Want to fast-track your self-awareness rather than working through it week by week
  • Are a clinician or helping professional seeking your own deep personal work

Details

  • Format: 3 sessions, approximately 2 hours each
  • Scheduling: Flexible — sessions can be spread across 1–2 weeks based on your availability
  • Location: In-person in Charlotte, NC or virtual
  • Includes: Full MARI® assessment, discovery and parts mapping, somatic integration session, Self-Discovery Journal, and personalized plan for moving forward
  • Led by: Alynne Davis, MA, LCMHC
