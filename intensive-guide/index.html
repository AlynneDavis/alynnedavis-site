5 Signs You’re Ready for
Deeper Healing Work

For women who have already done the work — and sense that something deeper is still waiting to be uncovered.

  • You’ve done the work — but something still feels unfinished. Things have shifted. But there’s still a part of you circling the same patterns, no matter how much awareness you bring to them.
  • You can talk about it, but you can’t feel your way through it. You understand what happened. But understanding hasn’t made it feel different in your body. The gap between knowing and feeling hasn’t closed.
  • You’re exhausted by the effort of holding it together. Underneath the functioning is a version of you that is quietly tired — tired of coping, tired of keeping the lid on, tired of performing okayness.
  • You’re at a turning point and you want real clarity. You don’t want more coping tools. You want to go deep, move through it, and walk away knowing yourself in a way you didn’t before.
  • You sense the answers are already inside you. You’re not looking for someone to fix you. You’re looking for a space that trusts you to lead your own healing.

If you recognized yourself in even one of these — this guide was written for you.

Alynne Davis, LCMHC Expressive Arts Therapist • Charlotte, NC
