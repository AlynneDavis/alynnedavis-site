Therapy Services

Trauma-informed expressive arts therapy for women in Charlotte, NC. Explore the areas of support I offer and find the path that feels right for you.

How We Can Work Together

Many women come to therapy feeling disconnected from themselves, overwhelmed by anxiety or perfectionism, or stuck in patterns around food, control, or relationships. My therapy services are designed to support women in rebuilding trust with themselves through relational, trauma-informed, and body-centered approaches.

Whether you are seeking weekly therapy, expressive arts sessions, or a focused healing intensive, we can create a path forward that meets you where you are.

Areas I Specialize In

My clinical work focuses on supporting women navigating complex relationships with their bodies, emotions, and identity. I specialize in trauma-informed therapy, eating disorder recovery, ADHD in women, body image healing, and major life transitions.

Each of the areas below represents a core focus of my work and reflects specialized training and experience supporting women in these challenges.

Eating Disorder Therapy

Specialized treatment for binge eating, bulimia, ARFID, orthorexia, and disordered eating patterns. I understand the complexity of food, control, and identity, and I approach recovery with both clinical expertise and deep compassion.

Learn More

Trauma Therapy

Trauma-informed therapy for women navigating the effects of childhood experiences, relational trauma, complex PTSD, and adverse life events. We work at a pace that honors your nervous system and builds genuine safety from the inside out.

Learn More

ADHD Therapy for Women

ADHD in women is frequently undiagnosed or masked by anxiety, perfectionism, and people-pleasing. I help women understand their ADHD, develop self-compassion, and build strategies that work with their brain rather than against it.

Learn More

Binge Eating Therapy

Binge eating is not about willpower. It is a response to something deeper. Therapy helps you understand what is driving the cycle, reconnect with your body, and build a relationship with food grounded in trust instead of fear.

Learn More

Expressive Arts Therapy

Creative modalities like art, movement, writing, and sensory exploration offer pathways to healing that go beyond words. Expressive arts therapy is especially powerful for women healing from trauma, grief, and disconnection from self.

Learn More

Self-Discovery Intensives

A guided 3-session experience using the MARI assessment for women ready to go deeper. Includes creative assessment, parts mapping, somatic integration, and a Self-Discovery Journal to take home.

Session Details and Logistics

In-Person and Virtual Options

I offer both in-person sessions in Charlotte, NC and virtual therapy sessions for clients located anywhere in North Carolina and South Carolina. Virtual sessions are conducted through a secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth platform, providing the same quality of care from the comfort of your own space.

Insurance and Payment

I am an out-of-network provider. This means I do not bill insurance directly, but I provide a superbill (detailed receipt) after each session that you can submit to your insurance company for potential out-of-network reimbursement. Many clients are surprised to find they receive significant reimbursement through their plans.

To make filing easier, you are also welcome to use Mentaya, a free tool that checks your out-of-network benefits and submits claims on your behalf. You can use your superbill with Mentaya or file directly with your insurance yourself — whichever works best for you.

I accept payment via credit card, debit card, and HSA/FSA cards. Payment is due at the time of each session.

If cost is a concern, I encourage you to reach out. I want to make sure the right support is accessible to you.

Getting Started

The first step is a free 15-minute phone consultation. During this call, you will have the opportunity to share a bit about what you are going through, ask any questions, and get a sense of whether my approach feels right for you. From there, we will schedule your first full session and begin.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which therapy service is right for me?

Many clients begin with a free consultation so we can talk about what you are experiencing and determine the most supportive approach. Whether that involves weekly therapy, expressive arts sessions, or a focused intensive, we will decide together what best supports your needs.

Do I need to have a diagnosis to start therapy?

No diagnosis is required to begin therapy. Many women seek support simply because they feel overwhelmed, disconnected from themselves, or stuck in patterns that are no longer working.

Do you offer therapy in person or online?

I offer both in-person therapy at my Charlotte, North Carolina office and secure virtual sessions for clients located anywhere in North Carolina and South Carolina.

Do you accept insurance?

I am an out-of-network provider. I provide a superbill after each session that you can submit to your insurance company for possible reimbursement depending on your plan. You can also use Mentaya, a free tool that checks your benefits and handles claim submission for you.
