Embodied Reclamation Institute  ·  Signature Program

Embodied Nourishment

A 6-Month 1:1 Coaching Experience for women ready to stop fighting their bodies and finally come home to themselves.

Schedule a Discovery Call

You’ve been here before. The cycle of restriction and overindulgence. The guilt that follows. The quiet war you’ve been waging against your own body for longer than you can remember.

You know, somewhere underneath it all, that this isn’t really about food. But knowing that hasn’t made it easier to stop.

Embodied Nourishment is a six-month 1:1 coaching experience designed for women who are tired of living in cycles of dieting, food guilt, body shame, emotional eating, perfectionism, and self-criticism — and who are ready for something different.

Not another program to white-knuckle your way through. Not more rules to follow from the outside in. Something grounded in who you actually are and what your body actually needs.

Through a compassionate, embodied approach, we work together to restore trust in your body, reconnect with your needs, and create a sustainable relationship with nourishment, movement, and self-care. Using the principles of Remember. Return. Reclaim.

This Might Be For You If

You recognize yourself here.

  • You feel disconnected from your body and struggle to trust what it’s telling you
  • You have a long history of dieting, food rules, or chronic preoccupation with what you’re eating
  • You struggle with emotional eating, stress eating, or using food to cope with overwhelm
  • Body image concerns quietly run in the background of your daily life
  • You want a more peaceful, sustainable relationship with food — one that doesn’t require willpower or perfection
  • You’re ready to cultivate genuine self-trust and self-compassion, not just manage symptoms
  • You desire deeper alignment with your values, your needs, and who you actually are
  • You’ve tried other approaches and they haven’t lasted — because they didn’t address what was underneath

The Framework

Remember. Return. Reclaim.

Embodied Nourishment is built on the RRR Method™ — a framework for moving through awareness, embodiment, and integration at your own pace, in your own way.

Phase 01

Remember

We begin by getting honest about the story. The messages you absorbed about your body. The coping patterns that developed for good reason. The shame that has quietly been running things. This is about finally seeing clearly — so it no longer drives from the shadows.

Phase 02

Return

We bring you back into your body. Through embodied and mindful practices, attention to nourishment and self-care, and the slow rebuilding of body trust — we create the conditions for a different relationship with food, movement, and yourself to actually take root.

Phase 03

Reclaim

This is where integration happens. You begin to inhabit your life differently — eating with more presence, moving with more ease, treating yourself with the compassion you’ve always extended to others. Not because you forced it. Because you remembered who you are.

Your Six-Month Container

What’s Included

Six Months of 1:1 Coaching

A dedicated six-month container built around you, your history, and your pace. Enough time to go beneath the surface and actually create something that lasts.

Bi-Weekly Sessions (12 Total)

60-minute 1:1 sessions held twice a month — or weekly in the beginning if that support level feels right. Each session is grounded in where you are right now.

Text Support Between Sessions

Access to me between sessions for brief questions, reflections, and encouragement. Available Monday through Friday during business hours. (See note below on boundaries.)

Embodiment & Mindfulness Practices

Somatic, creative, and self-reflection practices woven through our work together — to help you access and process what words alone can’t always reach.

Resources, Worksheets & Exercises

Personalized materials shared between sessions — reflection prompts, practices, and tools tailored to your goals and where you are in the process.

Personalized Support & Guidance

This isn’t a curriculum you move through on a preset schedule. It is built around you — your nervous system, your story, your becoming.

A Note on Text Support

Text support is available between sessions for brief check-ins, questions, reflections, and encouragement. Messages are responded to during normal business hours and are not intended for crisis support, emergency situations, or therapeutic processing.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988) or go to your nearest emergency room.

What Makes This Different

Clinical Depth. Coaching Container.

This is not a program with a meal plan at the center. It is not “wellness coaching” dressed in soft language. And it is not about getting you to eat differently through more discipline or willpower.

This is coaching led by someone who has spent over a decade in the room with women navigating eating struggles, body image concerns, and the deeper patterns underneath — and who has built a methodology from that work.

You will be guided by someone who holds:

  • Licensure as a Clinical Mental Health Counselor (LCMHC, LPC)
  • Training as an Expressive Arts Therapist
  • Certification as a MARI Practitioner
  • Certification as a Nutritionist
  • Healing Embodied Practitioner certification (in progress — international)

That depth means this work is nuanced, body-aware, and held with genuine care — while remaining clearly within a coaching container, distinct from therapy.

Please note: Coaching is not therapy and does not constitute mental health treatment. If you are seeking therapy services in North Carolina or South Carolina, please visit Alynne Davis, PLLC. If you are in crisis, please call or text 988.

In the Spirit of Honesty

Who This Is Not For

Embodied Nourishment is a coaching program, not a clinical treatment. It is not appropriate if you are currently in acute crisis, require medical monitoring for an eating disorder, need psychiatric support, or are seeking a clinical diagnosis or formal treatment.

If you are unsure whether coaching or therapy is the right next step for where you are right now, I’m happy to explore that with you on a discovery call — no obligation, just a conversation.
