A guide to understanding where you are right now — and what’s possible from here.

For the woman who knows something needs to shift — but isn’t quite sure where to begin.

Phase 01

Remember

“I lost myself somewhere along the way.”

Awakening to the story you’ve been living inside of — the patterns, the shame, the narrative that quietly ran things.

Phase 02

Return

“I know what’s happening — but I can’t feel my way through it.”

Coming home to your body. Rebuilding the capacity that survival mode depleted through nervous system work, nourishment, and somatic reconnection.

Phase 03

Reclaim

“I know who I’m becoming — I just haven’t stepped into her yet.”

Integration. Your voice returns. Your sense of self solidifies. You begin moving through your life as who you actually are.

Alynne Davis, LCMHC, LPC I’m a Licensed Clinical Mental Health Counselor, Expressive Arts Therapist, and the founder of the Embodied Reclamation Institute. I created the RRR Method from over a decade of clinical work with women navigating trauma, body struggles, neurodivergence, and identity loss. This guide is where that work begins.
